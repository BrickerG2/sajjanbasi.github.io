Info Tech

One of our most famous courses that students love to take is Info Tech.

Information and Communications Technology (ICT) have turned local communities into global communities. The kind of education that prepares students for this global community has technology as an integral part of its core. A technology-rich education enables students to bridge cultural and geographical distances around specific tasks, projects, information gathering and relationship building. It makes all subjects more meaningful to students and more adequately prepares them for the world in which they will live and work.
