Extra Curricular/After School Programs

At Enver Creek, we have many extra curricular or after school programs and all of our students have every right to participate in these activities. We play basketball, soccer, volleyball, and many other sports.

Our students are invited to participate in a variety of activities throughout the school year. Some sports are school-based and some involve meets, games or play days with other Surrey schools at various locations. We appreciate the ongoing support from our coaches, teachers, volunteers and parents as well as all our drivers!

Enver Creek Secondary School is a secondary school in Surrey, BC. It is located at 14505 84 Ave.
